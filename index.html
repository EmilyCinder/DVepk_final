Bio

Dark Velvet is an innovative music producer known for blending electronic beats with organic textures. With over a decade in the industry, Dark Velvet has crafted a signature sound that resonates across multiple genres.

Beginning as a classically trained pianist, Dark Velvet transitioned into electronic music production, quickly gaining recognition for immersive sound design and cinematic compositions.
