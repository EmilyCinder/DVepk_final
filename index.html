Bio

Dark Velvet is based out of Sacramento, California, but is currently residing in Portland, Oregon. His project started about ten years ago and has been active with production and DJ’ing since. He has been a pioneer in his community in his production, leaving an undeniable presence and mark on the Sacramento area. Now residing in Portland, he works every day, perfecting his craft in Ableton and experimenting with new sound design techniques, and providing deliverables and sound packs for the community.
